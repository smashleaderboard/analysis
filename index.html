Methodology

Projected points are estimated using consensus predictions from Opta's 25,000-simulation supercomputer, FanDuel/DraftKings betting markets, and expert analysis from ESPN, Yahoo Sports, RotoWire and CBS Sports. For each country, the most likely exit round was identified and group-stage wins were estimated based on each team's strength relative to their group opponents.

Win probabilities for each sweepstake participant are modelled across each country's range of possible outcomes — not just the most likely scenario — weighted by tournament probability.

* Pre-tournament projection for entertainment purposes only. Football is wonderfully unpredictable — upsets, injuries and VAR controversies may render all of the above completely wrong.
